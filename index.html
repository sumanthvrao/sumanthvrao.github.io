About Me

I am a MS candidate at the University of California - San Diego in the Computer Science and Engineering department. I am broadly interested in efforts to improve security and privacy in technology systems. At UC San Diego, my master's thesis research is advised by Stefan Savage and Geoff Voelker, and I am a member of the SysNet research group. In my spare time, I keep busy with several hobbies - hiking, photography, and jogging to name a few. My hometown is Bengaluru, India.
