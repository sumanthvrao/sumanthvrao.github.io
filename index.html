About Me

I am a CS PhD student at UC San Diego advised by Stefan Savage and Geoff Voelker. My research focuses on improving security and privacy through empirical means. I am also a research intern at Corelight, in the Corelight Labs team. At UCSD, I'm a part of the Sysnet and CryptoSec research groups. My hometown is Bengaluru, India.
