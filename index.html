About Me

I'm a MS student in Computer Science at UC San Diego. I am broadly interested in efforts to improve security and privacy in technology systems. In the past I worked on examing Consumer mobile spyware apps to identify a range of failures in protecting user data and privacy. I am currently pursuing my Master's thesis co-advised by Stefan Savage and Geoffrey M Voelker and am a member of the SysNet group.
